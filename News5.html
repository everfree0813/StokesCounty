NEW GREEN BOX SITE HOURS

  • The Lawsonville, Sandy Ridge, Pine Hall, Hwy 66, and Francisco Solid Waste sites will be open on Wednesdays once again from 7:00 AM until 7:00 PM beginning April 3rd 2019.
  • The Pinnacle and Walnut Cove Solid Waste sites will remain open on Fridays until 9:00 PM beginning April 5th, 2019. Once daylight savings time ends in November each year, they will begin remaining open on Fridays until 8:00 PM and then go back to 9:00 PM again once daylight savings time begins in March each year.
